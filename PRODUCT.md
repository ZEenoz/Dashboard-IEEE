# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Municipal staff and the general public. They are checking the dashboard to monitor water levels, view reports, and stay informed about real-time environmental data.

## Product Purpose
To provide real-time water level and pressure monitoring data clearly and reliably, ensuring the community and officials are well-informed. Success is measured by how easily users (especially the elderly) can digest the information.

## Brand Personality
Modern, Accessible, and Easy to Use. The tone is informative, clear, and reassuring.

## Anti-references
Avoid overly technical, cluttered, or dense interfaces that might confuse the general public or elderly users. Avoid tiny text or low-contrast elements.

## Design Principles
1. **Mobile-First Clarity**: Design for the small screen first. Content must be digestible and perfectly legible on phones before considering the desktop layout.
2. **Accessible by Default**: High contrast, large touch targets, and legible typography that respects elderly users.
3. **Data Without Clutter**: Present complex IoT data (charts, maps, numbers) in a simplified, distilled manner without overwhelming the user.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Needs exceptionally clear typography and spacing for elderly users.
- High contrast ratios for all critical text and data points.
- Easy to tap buttons and navigation (large touch targets) for mobile users.
